Beautiful Dream

Download Game

Creative Vision

This is an extremely personal narrative work, full of details and fragments from my memory. Maybe in the eyes of others, it seems a little hazy, even inexplicable, but this is exactly the meaning of its existence - to evoke those emotions that are indescribable and inexplicable, but truly exist. As I always insist: walk your own path and decide your own life.

Game Description

You open your eyes and find yourself back in 2015 - the familiar classroom, young classmates, and young self, everything seems to have never changed. Did you travel from the reality ten years later, or is this just a dream about the future?

In this short and gentle time travel, you will make your own choice between memories and reality.

Escape, Escape the Room

Download Game

Creative Vision

As a fan of suspense films, escape rooms have always been my favorite theme. This is also a more personal narrative. Whether it is the kitten, teacup, or notes, they all contain my ingenuity. There are many things I want to expand, but the content is limited, so it may seem simple.

Game Description

You find yourself trapped in a room with strange facilities and tables and chairs everywhere. Other creatures in this room guide you out, but the mysterious notes and 100-year-old trees hint at the intersection of time and space. So, did you find the way? Did you get out?
